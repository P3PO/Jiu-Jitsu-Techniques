Tips

  • When passing the guard, keep their hips and back on the ground.

  • Triangle -> Leg points to ground not back. Don't sit back.
    When have one knee inside their legs. The inside knee should be up, the other one down. Never both down. Can then step over and start to take the side.
    When their hand on your lapel, careful other hand doesnt come round for choke.
    When they have you in butterfly guard, keep knees tight so they have no lift.
    Grapevine -> Make foot point to the sky.
    When knee over, keep toes in to avoid half guard.
