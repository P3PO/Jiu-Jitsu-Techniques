Kesa Gatame Attacks

  • In Kuzure Kesa Gatame. Hip under his armpit. Right arm underarm. Right leg forward. Left leg up.
    Left hand get his right wrist and move it down. Right leg forward, move his wrist under your right leg
    Triangle your left leg. Lift hip. Just move their arm down, move leg up, then bring it back.

    In Kuzure Kesa Gatame. His right arm under your right leg. Pull his right arm with your left so he can't take your back
    when you switch right underarm to overarm. Put right hand on ground for support. Roll over his left shoulder
    Your right leg over his head and into triangle.

  • Jackson Sousa: In Kuzure Kesa Gatame. Left and right arms underarm. Use left arm underarm to push
    their right arm across their face and force them to go onto their side. Right leg over.
    Sit back. Left leg hook. Take back.
