Opponent has you in half guard defence

  • Clock choke. Right hand grab lapel, weight on his shoulder, step around.

  • Opponent in turtle. You have their back. Right hand goes under their neck(over their shoulder)
    and grab their left lapel tight. Right leg go over their head and fall back over their shoulder 90 degrees
    Right leg over their neck - push down and pull with hand to choke. Left leg don't get trapped.
    Can grab their left arm and pull with your left to make the technique tighter.
