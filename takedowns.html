Takedowns

  • Double Leg: One leg up on the outside. Same side head goes on the outside. E.g. Left leg - head on left side
    Can pull and go straight back or pull hard on the outside leg and little tug on the other leg to the side.

  • O Soto Gari -> Left foot not too far, knee bent + attacking. Same level as uke's foot. Weight forward.
    Foot pointing straight. Reap knee to knee pointed. Left arm pulls down, use hip.
    O uchi Gari to pop their leg, then push and into o soto gari.
    Vs left hander: Left arm pull towards your belly. Right push against their chin. Hook knee, left leg
    in direction of the throw. Arms pull hard.
    Vs Right: Semicircle backstep. Change direction 45 degrees. If reap not working, can muscle through with o soto o-toshi.
    Left armpit, pull back, left leg steps back so right foot is forward. Grab belt, when he tries to go up, reap -> Ushiro Kesa Gatame.

  • Good Seoi Combo: First circle step seoi nage. Your left hand on his left lapel. Right hand cross grip, go over your left hand
    Circle step - left leg forward. Pull with arms like swinging something. Then right step and throw.

  • Yoko Tomoe Nage - Right vs left. Your right leg is forward. Put your left leg on their left hip. Fall down with your head
    going to their right foot. Lapel hand pushes towards their head. Sleeve hand in other direction. Kick with legs.

  • Rolling UchiMata: He grabs your right lapel. Your right arm goes around and traps their arm and grabs their right lapel.
    Hook their left leg with your right. Put left hand on the floor for support. Push with your shoulder, and throw in direction
    that they're facing.

  • Tani Otoshi: Lapel down. Sleeve to their hip. Right leg step to their left leg. Left leg follows close. Head on their chest and attack straight back.
