Attacks from Side Control

  • Mounting: From side control (knees not too close, one under his armpit), one hand grabs his far leg.
    Your corresponding leg goes back a bit to help swing over. Keep control of his leg + shoulder pressure to
    his face. Go over into mount. Knees under his armpits. Tip: Grab outside of his far leg and pull
    Bring your right leg back, and left leg forward. Don't let go off lef til foot over.

  • In side control, with left knee down and right knee up. They turn into turtle. Stay close, follow with your leg.
    and get the hook in, then the other hook.

  • Armbar from side control sitting up: Right underhook his left arm. Left arm round his head. Right knee goes to his belly.
    Left knee to his head. Both arms on his elbow, his hand on your shoulder. Pull with arms + push with upper body

  • Side control into choke: Left hand grab under his head, collar deep, grab gi and pull to other side. right hand control on outside of leg
    Left knee goes over to the right side of his face.
    Stretch out left leg. Knee pushes his head in one direction, while elbow on the floor goes round and pulls in the other direction.

  • Kimura from side control: Left arm round his head. Right arm gets his left arm, put your elbow on the centreline.
    Move into north south, left knee over his head, and right knee down. Turn him to the side, with left knee pressed
    against their shoulder. Foot not too close. First break grip - fingers are the weakest part. Kimura - put
    elbow on your chest and move whole body.

  • Armbar from side control: Left arm round their head. Right hand control their left elbow. Move elbow in
    close to their neck. Left hand lift their head. Left leg over. Calve under their head like a pillow
    Keep arm controlled. Right knee over - put head on the floor. Leave space between you and their arm
    Squeeze knees and control elbow - life.

  • Triangle from side control: Move knees left towards his head, until left knee near his head + control his right arm.
    Your right knee over his body. When he tries to stop it, grab his arm and swing leg around and under his head
    Can try to do triangle straight away or left arm grab your foot. Roll over right shoulder + adjust triangle.
    When triangle and no space - shrimp + lift hips.

  • Left hand under his head. Right arm control his left arm. Get his tricep, put right elbow on his belly. Left hand on floor
    for support. Raise their body a bit. Left leg swings round. First need to raise hip, put left foot under their body. Right leg
    over their face. Sit back and into armbar.

  • Henrique Lima Kimura: Your right hand grab their forearm(instead of classical where grab their wrist) and press their forearm
    down -> keep it pinned to the ground. Other hand goes under and grabs your own wrist. But then make your wrist go up like
    picking something up. (Like Fuk Sao but with wrist facing the ceiling). Also bring elbow a bit down.

  • Baseball choke. Right hand under their head. Grab behind their neck with Julian special grip. Palm down
    Left knee on their belly/hip to control their body down. Left hand palm up grab their right lapel
    Left arm straighten, bring elbows together, left arm press against their neck. If dont tap, can move towards their head.
    Variation: grab their left gi out of their belt. Pass to right hand, grab at the tip. Left hand grab under, baseball grip
    and do the same.
