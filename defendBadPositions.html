Defend Bad Positions

  • When you're in omoplata, roll and take side control.

  • When escaping the mount and his knees on your armpits. Push his hips and move back. Create space first
    Otherwise, too easy for them to escape your leg trap.

  • You on your back, they're standing. Grab cross lapel with your right arm, and left arm back for support.
    They try to step around: you follow. When opponent passes both legs to your right -> pull hard with your right arm
    to take them down into turtle. Right hand goes on floor, left arm goes round to take back cross lapel. Left knee up. right knee down.
