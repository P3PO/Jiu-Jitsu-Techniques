Judo

  • When passing the guard, keep their hips and back on the ground.

  • Holdowns:
    Kesa Gatame
    Kuzure Kesa Gatame
    Ushiro Kesa Gatame : Stay upright
    Kami Shiho Gatame : Keep belt tight + pull into yourself. Put pressure on their head with your chest + keep hips low to ground. Dig chin+ straight legs
    To escape, grab belt and inner thigh and pull towards your head
    Tate Shiho Gatame: Tuck heels under opponents butt.
    Yoko Shiho Gatame: Chest to chest. Head forward, looking up.
    Mune Gatame
    San Kaku Gatame

  • Turnovers:
    2 arms grab their arm, turn into yoko
    Sleeve and Lapel into Kesa Gatame
    In turtle, they grab opposite, roll into Ushiro
    On their back. Belt grab, into Kami
    Huizinga Bearhug roll
    Scissor Sweep
    Simon - grab armpit + own sleeve
    Opponent in turtle. Right arm grabs right lapel. Pin their left shoulder down, then swing around. Pull lapel. Turnover into Kami

  • Chokes:
    Superman, San Kaku Jime against turtle.
