Open Guard

  • Defence against the leg drag: He leg drags your right foot. Put your left foot flat on the ground
    Right arm on their left knee. Left arm on their left shoulder. Push to keep them away + shrimp to the left.
    Left leg goes to their left shoulder. Start to roll over your shoulder. Grab inside of their left leg to
    carry on rolling. Foot on their shoulder goes down to their hip. Keep rolling over shoulder. Right leg end
    up De La Riva. From outside of his leg -> hook on the inside.

  • You're on your back with them infront. De La Riva(or just hook) their right leg. Control their right arm with
    your right. Left arm grab their right foot. Go to the side. Lift hip. Wrap left arm deep around their foot.
    And put hand on your own belly. Then put right leg under their left knee and go back to being straight infront.
    Push with right leg + left leg which is on the outside of their right leg. Keep left leg tight + press down and
    push forward. Go back on floor and lift hips -> foot lock.
