Guard Pass Defence

  • You on your back. Opponent trying to guard pass and put you into side control. He's passing to your right.
    Hip escape to your left. Bring right knee in. Left leg go up high, then across and put on their left hip
    Shrimp back straight. Use your hands to keep them away when doing it. Also if they're low on your body
    might need to bring your foot close to your body.

  • Trick - They're passing your guard to your right. Turn left into turtle. Keep left elbow on left knee
    tight so they can't get hooks in. Grab their right leg with your hand so they cant go over
    If their hand goes round to grab under your right arm, you need to
    push their shoulder first. Roll back and recover guard.
